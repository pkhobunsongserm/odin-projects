Italian Leafy Greens Salad

Description

When doing three-course meals, it is important to balance the menu. Having a light salad as an entree that is easy to wip up will allow you to focus your time on the main or dessert, whichever you want to make it shine.

While this recipe does call for grapeseed oil, olive oil will use just as well, if not easier to find.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups romaine lettuce - torn, washed and dried
  • 1 cup torn escarole
  • 1 cup torn radicchio
  • 1 cup torn red leaf lettuce
  • 12 cherry tomatoes
  • ½ red bell pepper, sliced into rings
  • ½ green bell pepper, sliced in rings
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions

For the dressing:

  • ¼ cup grapeseed oil or olive oil
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine romaine, escarole, radicchio, red leaf lettuce, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and green onions in a large bowl.
  2. Make dressing: Whisk together grapeseed oil, vinegar, lemon juice, basil, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over salad, toss well, and serve immediately.
